Privacy Policy – Review Assist

Last updated: August 2025

Review Assist is a Chrome extension designed to streamline content review workflows with tools for timestamp copying and caption analysis. We respect your privacy, and this extension is built to operate entirely within your browser.

Data Collection

  • We do not collect, transmit, or sell any user data.
  • All processing (timestamp copying, caption scanning, and word categorization) happens locally in your browser.
  • No browsing history, personal data, or page content is sent outside of your device.

Data Storage

  • User configurations (such as site settings, timestamp selectors, caption selectors, and custom word categories) are stored using Chrome’s storage.local.
  • This data is stored only on your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Permissions

  • Storage: Used only to save your local configuration and word lists.
  • Scripting & activeTab: Used only when you interact with the extension to inject the tools on approved pages.
  • Optional Host Permissions: You choose which sites the extension can access. The extension only operates on the pages you explicitly allow.

Third Parties

  • Review Assist does not communicate with external servers.
  • No data is shared with third-party services or advertisers.

Your Control

  • You can remove all stored data at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing the extension’s data via Chrome settings.
  • You can manage which sites Review Assist has access to via Chrome’s “Site access” settings.
